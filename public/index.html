The Corporate Development Challenge

In most companies, development teams struggle with unclear user stories, leading to delays, rework, and inefficiency.

Unclear Requirements

Vague acceptance criteria and missing domain knowledge cause confusion and multiple iterations.

Poor Estimation

Difficulty in story estimation leads to missed deadlines and unrealistic sprint commitments.

Hidden Blockers

Unidentified risks and dependencies cause unexpected surprises during development.
